At its core, "Road to Perdition" is a character-driven narrative that explores the complexities of morality and the consequences of one's actions. Michael Sullivan, portrayed with haunting intensity by Tom Hanks, grapples with the weight of his past sins and the desire to protect his son from the darkness that consumes him. As the film unfolds, we witness Sullivan's transformation from a hardened criminal to a man driven by a sense of duty and redemption, willing to sacrifice everything for the sake of his son's future.
