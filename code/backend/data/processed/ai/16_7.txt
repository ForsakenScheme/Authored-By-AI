In conclusion, "Lord of the Flies" is a profound literary work that delves deep into the heart of human nature. Through the unfolding narrative, Golding's exploration of the conflict between civilization and savagery, the character development, and the symbolic elements, the novel leaves readers with a haunting reflection on the true nature of humanity. It is a literary masterpiece that continues to resonate with readers, prompting them to contemplate the dark facets of human behavior when society's veneer is peeled away.
